History

Since I was little, my dad took my four siblings and me rafting. Those were the best moments of adventure and, above all, family. I remember having so much fun that we spend weeks dreaming about those experiences. For us, rafting means family and beautiful memories.

That`s why Mako Rafting was born in 2015. A family business created to share a piece of our childhood with those who want to enjoy and connect, just like we did. And yes, Mako is one of the fastest sharks just like rafting: extreme speed, fun and unforgettable adventure.
